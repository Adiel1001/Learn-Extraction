# 🧠 AI Second Brain

**Your intelligent business companion that compounds knowledge with every project.**

This repository is a systematic approach to capturing, organizing, and compounding your business knowledge. Every project you complete makes the next one faster and better.

---

## 🎯 How This Works

The Second Brain operates on a simple principle: **capture once, reuse infinitely**.

```
Memory (semantic knowledge) + Experiences (episodic memory) = Compounding intelligence
```

### The 4-Command Workflow

```
/plan [task]  →  /work [plan]  →  /review [output]  →  /learn
    ↓               ↓                  ↓                 ↓
 Research       Execute with       6-agent           Extract
 + Plan         todo tracking      quality check     patterns
```

Every cycle makes your brain smarter. Patterns extracted from one project accelerate the next.

## 🚀 Quick Start

### First Time Setup (10 minutes)
1. **Fill in your context**: Edit `CLAUDE.md` with your name, role, and business info
2. **Run setup**: `/setup` for guided configuration
3. **Add company basics**: Fill `memory/company/` with your products and positioning

### Daily Workflow (3 minutes per task)
1. **Start with plan**: `/plan [task description]`
2. **Execute systematically**: `/work [plan.md]`
3. **Quality check**: `/review [output.md]`
4. **Extract wisdom**: `/learn`

### Need Quick Help?
```
/ask "Help me write a follow-up email to a prospect"
```

That's it. Your brain is working.
