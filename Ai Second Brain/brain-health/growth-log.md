## Understanding Growth Velocity

### Healthy Growth Patterns

- **1-2 entries/week**: Steady, sustainable growth
- **3-5 entries/week**: Rapid learning phase
- **Daily entries**: Intensive project period

### Signs of Healthy Brain

1. Regular `/learn` cycles (not skipped)
2. Patterns progressing through confidence levels
3. Improving review scores over time
4. Decreasing time on similar tasks

### Warning Signs

1. Long gaps between entries
2. Patterns stuck at LOW confidence
3. Declining or stagnant review scores
4. Skipping `/learn` after projects
